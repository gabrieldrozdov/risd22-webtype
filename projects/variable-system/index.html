Welcome to the
WIKI TOWNSHIP

This project reimagines an online database (i.e. Wikipedia) as a virtual town, where types of pages are places located in a pseudo-physical spacetime thingamajig. The individual pages are then spaces (or people) within those places. And when you click a link to see a page, the sounds from the town remain with you.

In this town, you will meet people, hear conversations, witness art and knowledge, and observe governmental happenings.
