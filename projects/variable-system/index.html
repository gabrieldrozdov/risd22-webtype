Welcome to the WIKI TOWNSHIP

Please view on a desktop with your sound on!

This project reconceptualizes an online database (i.e. Wikipedia) as a virtual town, where types of pages are places located in a pseudo-physical spacetime thingamajig. The individual pages are then spaces (or people) within those places.
