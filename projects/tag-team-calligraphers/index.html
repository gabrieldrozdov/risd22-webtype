Tag Team Calligaphers

This is a tag team project between you and a robot.

Click below to start drawing.
Watch out — there’s a timer!

After you finish your drawing, the robot will do its best to trace your image.
Please be kind, it isn’t the best at this.

Once the robot is done, trace its tracing of your drawing.
Rinse and repeat.

Watch out — you’ll have less time to draw each time!
Your tag teaming ends after 5 drawings.

You'll receive a digital image of your work as a prize.
If you’d be so kind, please email this image to me.

Thanks!
